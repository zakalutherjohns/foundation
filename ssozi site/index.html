About Us

God is Able Academic Foundation is committed to uplifting underprivileged communities by providing access to quality education, healthcare, and mental wellbeing programs. We believe in creating opportunities for everyone to thrive.

Objectives

• To provide basic medical requirements to the needy.
• To provide counselling services to those in need.
• To sensitize and educate people about health and education.
